About Us

FONAH foundation is a philanthropic body of FONAH NETWORK, an entity registered with Corporate Affairs Commission BN: 2979343. The foundation was officially inaugurated on 1st of October,2019 by youths who realized the necessity of its establishment as a means of contributing to their community in order to achieve sustainable developments in line with the United Nations sustainable developmental goals SDGs in their community and country at large.

The foundation is a non-governmental initiative aimed at making innovative changes that would bring about personal and societal growth. The foundation is a community of active youth who are ready to add values and proffer sustainable solution to seemingly unsolvable problems in their immediate environment and the country at large.

The need to make positive changes is very crucial, and cannot be overemphasized as we are faced with daunting challenges that have eaten deep into the system of the nation. To improve and upgrade our community in both urban and rural areas through community based advocacy in all possible areas, effective measures need to be put in place.

At FONAH foundation, we are driven by passion to make meaningful and effective contribution that would positively change the sad narrative.
